Smart plugs · Switches · App discontinued — Manual control only
📴
App Support Discontinued — Circa 2023 Belkin shut down the Wemo cloud infrastructure and app ecosystem. These devices no longer connect to any app or voice assistant. They function as manual switches only. Some limited third-party workarounds exist via Home Assistant local integration, but severely limit functionality. Replacement with an active brand is recommended.
